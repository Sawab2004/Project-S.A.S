#Project S.A.S
Genre: Action / Survival / Shooter
Mode: Single Player
🧟‍♂️ 1. Game Overview

Project S.A.S is an action-packed survival shooter where players must navigate a hostile, zombie-infested terrain. With limited visibility and scarce resources, players must rely on strategy, awareness, and quick reactions to survive.

🧭 2. Game Concept & Storyline
Short Description

Project S.A.S is a fast-paced survival game set in a dangerous quarantine zone overrun by zombie-like infected creatures. Players explore an abandoned landscape, manage critical resources, and fight off enemies while maintaining visibility in a dark, tense atmosphere.

Story Summary

Following a catastrophic outbreak, human civilization has collapsed. You play as a lone Special Assault Soldier (S.A.S operative) deployed on a final mission to eliminate every infected threat within the quarantine zone.
Armed with limited supplies and a weakening flashlight, you must search the terrain, recover resources, and survive long enough to clear the area.

Main Objective

✔️ Eliminate all zombies in the terrain without dying.

Theme & Setting

Setting: Abandoned open-terrain environment

Era: Near-future post-apocalyptic world

Atmosphere: Dark, suspenseful, and filled with sudden danger

🎮 3. Gameplay & Mechanics
Core Gameplay Loop

Navigate the terrain

Search for ammo and battery pickups

Engage zombies using multiple weapons

Manage flashlight energy

Eliminate all threats to complete the mission

Player Controls
Action	Input
Movement	Keyboard
Camera	Mouse
Shoot / Aim	Mouse buttons
Switch Weapon	Mouse scroll wheel
Interact / Pickup	Keyboard key
Toggle Flashlight	Keyboard key
Rules & Challenges

Flashlight power decays and must be recharged using batteries found in the map.

Visibility decreases dramatically when the flashlight is depleted.

Zombies may patrol, wander, or chase based on AI states.

Limited ammo forces the player to explore the entire terrain.

Win / Lose Conditions

Win: Eliminate all zombies

Lose: Player health reaches zero

⭐ 4. Features

AI-controlled zombies with patrol & chase behavior

Flashlight system with battery decay

Multiple weapons: Pistol, Shotgun, Carbine Rifle

Ammo & battery pickups

Immersive UI (health, ammo, battery indicator)

Atmospheric sound effects & music

Terrain-based level with props and interactable items

🧍‍♂️ 5. Characters & Assets
Main Character (S.A.S Operative)

Equipped with multiple firearms

Has a flashlight with consumable battery

Abilities: shooting, sprinting, switching weapons, interacting

Enemies (Zombies)

Patrol or wander until detecting the player

Chase the player within detection range

Deal melee damage

More dangerous in darkness

Environment & Props

Trees, rocks, crates, abandoned structures

Ammo and battery pickups

Lighting, fog, and atmospheric elements

🖥️ 6. UI Screens

Main Menu: Start, Settings, Exit

HUD: Health bar, ammo counter, weapon icon, flashlight battery

Pause Menu: Resume, Settings, Exit
